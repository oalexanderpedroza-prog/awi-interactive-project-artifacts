What Happens at Each Stage

Researcher actions + outputs

Week 1 · Framing & Alignment

Define the problem

Translate a vague product request into a focused research direction tied to high-risk uncertainty and product decisions.

Researcher Actions

  • Review roadmap, prior research, and product constraints
  • Run assumptions workshop with PM, design, and engineering
  • Convert product questions into research questions
  • Define decision scope and success metrics

Deliverables

  • Research Brief
  • Hypothesis statements
  • Decision log entry
  • Risk vs. unknowns map

Week 2 · Study Design

Choose the method

Select defensible methods based on product maturity, uncertainty, timeline, and cost of being wrong.

Researcher Actions

  • Match questions to interviews, usability, survey, or logs
  • Define segments and recruitment strategy
  • Set sample sizes and fallback plans
  • Align rigor level with prototype maturity

Deliverables

  • Research Plan
  • Sample plan
  • Recruitment screener
  • Decision log update

Week 3 · Instruments

Prepare the study

Build the smallest, clearest set of research materials needed to generate the right behavioral signal.

Researcher Actions

  • Draft interview guide and XR task scenarios
  • Define success criteria and metrics
  • Pilot test with internal participants
  • Revise for clarity, fatigue, and timing

Deliverables

  • Discussion guide
  • Usability protocol
  • Survey instrument
  • Version-controlled final materials

Weeks 4–5 · Fieldwork

Capture real behavior

Run moderated sessions with prototype hardware and document behavior, friction, workload, and context effects.

Researcher Actions

  • Conduct onboarding, interview, task testing, and debrief
  • Observe hesitation, task errors, and adaptation patterns
  • Log fatigue, trust, and multitasking friction
  • Share interim updates to avoid stakeholder surprise

Deliverables

  • Session recordings
  • Transcripts and observation notes
  • Telemetry logs
  • Interim findings update

Week 6 · Synthesis

Turn data into insight

Move from raw observations to themes, behavioral mechanisms, and evidence-backed product implications.

Researcher Actions

  • Cluster notes and transcript evidence
  • Check AI-suggested themes against raw data
  • Identify cognitive load, trust, and ergonomics mechanisms
  • Prioritize 3–5 core insights

Deliverables

  • Affinity map
  • Insight statements
  • Journey or behavior map
  • Validated evidence links

Week 7 · Strategy Impact

Influence roadmap

Translate insights into design direction, prioritization decisions, and a clear set of next-step experiments.

Researcher Actions

  • Prioritize findings by severity, frequency, and strategic value
  • Run workshop with PM, design, and engineering
  • Connect insights to roadmap decisions
  • Mark hypotheses as validated, partial, or uncertain

Deliverables

  • Research readout deck
  • Opportunity areas
  • Design principles
  • Prioritized recommendations

Week 8 · Knowledge & Portfolio

Make it reusable

Convert project work into reusable organizational knowledge and portfolio-ready evidence of research skill.

Researcher Actions

  • Tag insights in the repository
  • Log open questions for future research cycles
  • Document tradeoffs and pivots
  • Translate work into case study narrative

Deliverables

  • Repository updates
  • Case study draft
  • Decision audit trail
  • Next-cycle research backlog
